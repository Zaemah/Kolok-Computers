About Us

Kolok Investments Pvt Ltd

Established in May 2021, Kolok Investments Pvt Ltd is a forward-thinking company born out of the realization that the demand for computers and related activities is rapidly escalating. This trend is fueled by the unprecedented pace of technological innovations and advancements, which have become an integral part of our daily lives.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading provider of computer and cartridge solutions in Zimbabwe, leveraging technological advancements to drive growth, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We aim to build long-lasting relationships with our customers, supporting them in their digital transformation journey.

Our Mission

is to cater to the growing demand for Information Technology (ITC) products and services, providing top-notch computer solutions and accessories to individuals, organizations, and businesses. We strive to bridge the gap between technology and our customers' needs, empowering them to thrive in an increasingly digital world.
